About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Government Engineering College, Khagaria, Bihar. With over 8 years of academic experience, I am passionate about teaching, research, and guiding the next generation of engineers.

My academic journey includes a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from IIT Patna, specializing in Power Systems and Renewable Energy Integration. I believe in creating an engaging learning environment that combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications.

My research focuses on developing sustainable energy solutions and improving grid stability in renewable-rich power systems. I actively collaborate with industry partners and academic institutions to address real-world engineering challenges.
