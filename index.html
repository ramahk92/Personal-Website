About

Prof. Ram Kumar is an accomplished researcher in the field of Electrical Engineering with a focus on power electronics, control systems, and renewable energy systems. He is currently guiding multiple PhD scholars and has a strong publication record.

Recent Projects

  • Solar EV Charging Circuit: Developed a three-phase solar-based EV charging system supporting G2V and V2G operation.
  • Grid-Connected Solar PV System: Modeled and simulated a three-phase solar PV system integrated with the utility grid.
  • Load Frequency Control: Implemented PID-based LFC for a two-area power system under various disturbances.
